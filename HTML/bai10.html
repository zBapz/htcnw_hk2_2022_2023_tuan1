IUH

The foreunner of the Industrial University Of Ho Chi Minh University was Professional Tranning School in Go Vap and founded Decenber 1996. March 1999, the school was upgraded to colleage Industry IV and December 2004 School was upgraded to Ho Chi Minh University of Industry under Decision No 214/2004/QD-TTg of the Prime Minister.
